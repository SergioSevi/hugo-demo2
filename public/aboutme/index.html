Sergio Sevillano

Postdoc at IPPP, Durham University

Hi, I'm Sergio Sevillano, a Spanish in the UK trying to learn some stuff about the world. This is my personal website, where you can find my academic carrer so far, latest publications, cv and talks/outreach I have done. My main interests lie in modified gravity, particularly from a particle standpoint. For that, I have developed a code to study the impact of scalar-tensor theories in the Standard Model. Moreover, I have also studied the fifth forces and screening of them from a particle perspective. I hope you find what you are looking for!
